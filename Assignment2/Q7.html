Event Overview

Join us this holiday season for a festive celebration of community spirit! The Christmas Community Event 2024 will be filled with joy, music, and holiday cheer. We invite everyone to gather at the Snowy Pines Park, located at North Pole, on Tuesday, 24 December 2024 from 10:00 AM to 6:00 PM.

Main Activities

  • Christmas Parade: Watch the festive floats and costumes as they light up the streets!
  • Santa's Grotto: Bring your little ones for a magical photo with Santa Claus.
  • Holiday Craft Fair: Shop for unique gifts, decorations, and handmade crafts from local artisans.
  • Caroling & Music: Enjoy live holiday music and carolers performing your favorite Christmas tunes.
  • Christmas Tree Lighting Ceremony: Join us for the grand lighting of the community Christmas tree at dusk!
  • Winter Wonderland Games: Fun activities for the whole family including a snowball fight arena and ice skating rink.
