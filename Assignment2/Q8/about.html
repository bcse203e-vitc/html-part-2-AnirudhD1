About Vandaloor Zoo

Vandaloor Zoo, established in 1855, is the first public zoo in Tamil Nadu. Spread over 602 hectares, it houses a wide variety of animals, birds, and reptiles. The zoo is dedicated to wildlife conservation, education, and public awareness. It is located in Vandaloor, about 15 km south of Chennai, and is one of the largest zoos in India.

Highlights of the Zoo

  • Home to over 2,500 animals representing more than 150 species.
  • Spread across 602 hectares, featuring a diverse range of habitats.
  • Conservation efforts focused on endangered species like the Bengal Tiger and Indian Lion.
  • Visitor-friendly facilities such as a mini-train, boating, and safari tours.
  • Research and educational programs to promote wildlife awareness.
