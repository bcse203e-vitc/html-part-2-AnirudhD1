Welcome to Vandaloor Zoo

Located on the outskirts of Chennai, Vandaloor Zoo, officially known as the Arignar Anna Zoological Park, is one of India's largest and most renowned zoos. Spread across 602 hectares, the zoo is home to more than 2,500 animals and birds, making it a popular destination for wildlife enthusiasts and families alike.
